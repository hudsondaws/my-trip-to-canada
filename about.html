Exploring Jasper & Banff

During my trip to Canada, I spent most of my time exploring the beautiful national parks of Jasper and Banff. The towering mountains, turquoise lakes, and wildlife made every moment unforgettable.

I hiked scenic trails, took countless photos, and enjoyed the peaceful nature that Canada is known for. The crisp mountain air and stunning sunsets made it feel like a dream come true!
